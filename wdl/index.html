AboutUs

Recommendation Hub, an Indian recommendation provider headquartered in Terna Engineering College, Mumbai, founded in 2019 by fellow Binge watchers Rishiraj Thadeshwar and Aswanth Nair in Marine Lines, Mumbai. Ever met someone with a frighteningly similar taste? We discovered that this is often the best person to suggest something you might like. Your recommendations here come from the collective souls of people who are extremely similar to you.
